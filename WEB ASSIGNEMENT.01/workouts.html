Workout Routines

Here are some effective workout routines to help you achieve your fitness goals:

Full Body Workout

This workout targets all major muscle groups.

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Squats: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Cardio Routine

A great way to boost your heart health.

  • Running: 30 minutes
  • Cycling: 30 minutes
  • Jump Rope: 5 minutes
